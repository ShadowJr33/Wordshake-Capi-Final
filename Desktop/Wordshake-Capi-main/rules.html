Rules

How to Play Wordshake

  1. You have 3 minutes to find as many words as possible.
  2. Words must be at least 3 letters long.
  3. Each letter can only be used once per word.
  4. Submit your words before the time runs out.
  5. Points are awarded based on the length of the word.

Scoring

The points are awarded based on the length of the word as follows:

  • 3-letter words: 1 point
  • 4-letter words: 2 points
  • 5-letter words: 3 points
  • 6-letter words: 4 points
  • 7-letter words: 5 points
  • 8 or more letters: 11 points

Try to find the longest words to maximize your score!
